About Feng Cha

Our passion for making tea extends far beyond our drinks. All of our stores strive their best to provide a comfy teahouse experience to our customers. In Feng Cha, we always working hard on finding good ingredients to make our quality drinks and fine pastries, even the music that you hear in store is carefully chosen to ensure a relaxing in-store experience. We are happy to see people of all ages, different backgrounds coming to Feng Cha to chat, hang out, study and work. We are thankful for each everyone's support and will continue working hard to provide a unique and cozy teahouse experience.
